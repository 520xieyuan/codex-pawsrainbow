Privacy Policy

Your memories stay protected.

Effective date: February 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PAWSRAINBOW LLC collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Paws Rainbow website and services.

Information We Collect

  • Account and contact details such as your email address.
  • Memorial content you provide, including pet names, dates, stories, and photos.
  • Usage data such as IP address, browser type, and access logs for security and abuse prevention.
  • Payment information is processed by Stripe and not stored directly by Paws Rainbow.

How We Use Information

  • Create and host memorial pages and deliver your magic link for edits.
  • Process payments and unlock permanent features.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve service quality.
  • Respond to support requests.

Data Retention

Free memorials are retained for 7 days unless upgraded. Paid memorials are retained as long as the service is offered and you do not request deletion.

Sharing and Processors

We use trusted processors to operate the service:

  • Stripe (payments)
  • Supabase (database and file storage)
  • Vercel (hosting)
  • Resend (email delivery)
  • Google Fonts (typography)

We do not sell your personal information.

Your Choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.
  • You can request removal of a memorial page at any time.
